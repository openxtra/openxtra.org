Length</h2><p>The standard specifies a maximum length of 90 metres for Permanent links and 100 metres for Channel links. It is possible that everything will work with longer lengths, but if you want to be sure that your installations will remain compliant with future standards then you should never exceed 90 metre cable lengths.</p><h2>Wiremapping</h2><p>In all cases all the pins must be properly connected.</p><h2>Propagation Delay</h2><p>Permanent link propagation delay at 10Mhz must not be greater than 498 nanoseconds.</p><p>Channel link propagation delay at 10Mhz must not be greater than 555 nanoseconds.</p><h2>Delay Skew</h2><p>Permanent link delay skew at 10Mhz must not be greater than 44 nanoseconds.</p><p>Channel link delay skew at 10Mhz must not be greater than 50 nanoseconds.</p>
